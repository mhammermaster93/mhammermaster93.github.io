I started playing around with network and tracert. I started becoming curious about how the world around me worked and I started learn and read more about it. I am hoping to dabble a little bit into engineering in the future, but for right now I am just going to play it safe and pick up arduino. I am currently attending college and I am half way through my diploma and then I hope to transfer to a school in the states.
